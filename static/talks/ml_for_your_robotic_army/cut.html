The 90% Rule

True for many fields

Each English word can be assigned a part-of-speech (POS) -- verbs, nouns, ...

Pierre|NNP Vinken|NNP , 61|CD years|NNS old|JJ , will|MD join|VB the|DT board|NN .

State-of-the-art accuracy is ~97% -- yet assigning the tag a word is most commonly seen with achieves 90%!
